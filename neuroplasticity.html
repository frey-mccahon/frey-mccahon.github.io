Neuroplasticity


Just understanding the nature of neuroplasicity can have an impact on one’s own neuroplasicity.


I have experienced this ’the self-fulfilling prophecy’ type phenomenon myself. When I have faith in my own ability to achieve a task, despite how difficult the task might be, I’ve noticed that it usually goes a lot better than when I approach the task with doubt or pessimism.

Since reading bout how just an awareness of neuroplasicity strengthens the growth of pathways between neurons at the beginning of the course, I feel like it has been working. Wether this is actually happening inside MY OWN brain chemistry is unknown, but regardless of that I believe this awareness has had a positive effect on my study practice.

Neuroplasicity is the actual physical growth and strengthening of neural pathways in the mind, the phenomenon of how an awareness of neuroplasicity reenforcing the process is related to the concept of a ‘growth mindset’ which refers to the idea that your brain’s abilities and capacities aren’t static but can expand. An awareness of these concepts are powerful elements in one’s suite of tools to assist learning, along with exercise, note taking and carful time management.
