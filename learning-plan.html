FREY-TECH / CORE / LEARNING PLAN

Learning Plan


1. What is your long term goal or career pathway?

I would ultimately like to work for myself. It’s my goal to accumulate enough programming skills to build a product I can successfully sell and profit off. As for what that pathway looks like just yet I can’t tell without gaining more visibility into the nature and landscape of the internet and computer programming.


2. A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

I think I have a strong creative instinct when I learn. This often leads to me finding some element of the task that I can make a lot of fun. Although this can aid the learning experience and keep me engaged, it can also serve to lead me down a rabbit hole and lose my pace with the curriculum.


3. What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I think everyone has emotional blindspots, even people who are considered to have high ‘E.Q’s’. I hope that working with others creatively in teams will help me learn where mine are and how best to develop an awareness of them.


4. A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme. This should include:

-An explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, Facilitators and industry/community representatives

I’ll try my best to be open minded and aware of the needs and wants of the people around me.

-What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you

I’ll take a moment to re-set. I often find that when you reach a certain capacity in study or working on a project, and frustration starts to arise, instinct often pushes you on to keep bashing against the problem. This is often counterintuitive, as it’s usually a more efficient use of your over all time to take a break and come back to it.


5. A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

I can get lost down rabbit holes, so if i can't find a solution quick enough, and it's not crucial i solve it in said moment, i'll make an effort to just make a note of it to research later and move on. If it is crucial I solve the problem now, and my internet searches don't come up fruitful I will message a friend or one of the discord channels.


6. A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

I expect the facilitators to help and encourage me as long as I am putting in a good effort.
