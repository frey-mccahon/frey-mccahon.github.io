FREY-TECH / CORE / BOOTCAMP LEARNING PLAN


What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

So far this hasn’t changed too much from my initial plan posted in my first Learning Plan Core blog. I ultimately want to design and develop my own products and programs. But I think acquiring the skills to do this will probably require me to get a job after Dev to refine my skills and knowledge in a real world working environment.


What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

It’s quite hard for me to confidently guess the answer to that at this point. My confidence in my technical ability is medium to low, so I think my strengths will be in coming up with concepts and ideas about the user end experience.


What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

Continuing to ask for help from people when I’ve already asked them many times. Getting over the awkwardness of asking people to go over concepts they have already gone over with me.


What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

My patience with myself. Developing a sense of when I should take break from solving a problem or change my modes of thinking in the aid of the greater project’s health, and my own psyche’s health.


What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

That they will be patient with me and believe in my potential further into the course. I’m hoping they will have a good instinct of when it’s a good time to step in and provide support and when to pull back and let me try crack a tough nut.


What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp?

To be patient with myself and not let fatigue dishearten me and negatively effect my development. I hope I can sort of stand back from myself and see when my focus is blurring and that I need to take a break to relieve some pressure, then come back in and get back into focus.
